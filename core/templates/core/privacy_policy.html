Privacy Policy

At My Legal Consultants, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

A. Personal Information
  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Address
  • Uploaded documents (if any)
  • Details provided during registration or consultation booking
B. Automatically Collected Data
  • IP address
  • Device and browser information
  • Cookies and session data
  • Pages visited and time spent
C. Sensitive Information (Optional)

If you voluntarily share legal case details, documents, or confidential information for consultation purposes, they remain protected and confidential.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide legal consultation and related services
  • To verify your identity and account
  • To improve platform functionality and user experience
  • To communicate regarding bookings, updates, or support
  • To prevent fraud, unauthorized activity, or abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. How We Protect Your Data

We use strong security measures including encryption, secure servers, admin-level access controls, and monitoring systems to protect your personal information.

However, no method of online transmission is 100% secure. While we take all reasonable precautions, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We may share your data only with:

  • Verified Advocates — to provide consultation
  • Service providers — such as hosting, email, or security partners
  • Legal authorities — if required under law

5. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to improve user experience, analyze website traffic, and personalize content. You may choose to disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Update or correct your data
  • Delete your account (subject to verification)
  • Withdraw consent for communication
  • Request deletion of uploaded documents

7. Retention of Data

We retain your information only as long as necessary for legal, business, or support purposes. Consultation-related documents may be stored for audit and compliance needs.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the content, practices, or privacy policies of these third-party sites.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals below 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes become effective once published on this page.
